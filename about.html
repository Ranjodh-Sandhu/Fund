About Us

Find out more about us and our vision for change

Our Vision

We are a company who stands on the values of generosity and integrity. As our result, we desire to be a medium of generosity between our investors and borrowers in order to make sure that nobody is missing the capital they need to improve their lives. As can seen by our logo, a coin and a pen, we aim to replace the tradiitonal bank eperience through our service.

What we do

We provide capital to qualified borrowers at better rates than the banks and with a farther reach than most traditional banks. With our risk varied options, we beleive that no potential borrower should be left out, which is why we assess each borrower as an individual. Using our fundd.me Complete Picture process, if you opt in, we aim to offer you a better rate by evaluating you as a person, not a number. On the other side, we believe that those who provide the capital for the benefit of our borrowers ought to see those benefits. As a result, we pass every penny of return to our investors, save for covering costs, and provide them with reliable diversified returns.

Our Beginnings

We are three students from the University of Virginia jointly with three computer science majors, two economics majors, and two business minors. Our interests lie in creating value at the intersection between debt financing and crowdfunding.
