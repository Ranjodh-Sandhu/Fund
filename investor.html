As an Investor

Learn more about how fundd can help you take control of your money.

Greater Returns

Traditionally, you deposit your money into banks where they then invest that money to make returns. You receive a small amount of interest, but it is minimal when comapred to the returns that the banks are making off of your hard-earned cash. By removing the banks from the equation, you can receive a much larger proportion of the returns instead of leaving the money in the hands of banks.

Smart Screening Technology

Our Smart Screening Technology analyzes data from all potential lenders in order to place them in different categories of risk. We use their credit scores, background, education, loan type, as well as many other factors in order to create our own score to place them in the appropriate category. This way, you can be more confident in the loans you fund.

Crowdsourced

Because the loans are crowdsourced, all of your money is not being placed into one loan. Instead, your money is distributed among many loans in the same risk category in order to lessen any risks of default.
